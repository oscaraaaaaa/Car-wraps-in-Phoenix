What Are Car Wraps?

Car wraps are large vinyl decals or graphics that are applied directly over the original paint of a vehicle. They can cover the entire car (full wrap) or specific sections (partial wrap), and they're designed not only to transform the appearance of a vehicle but also to protect its original paint.

Unlike paint jobs, which are permanent, wraps are removable and customizable, making them a popular option for both personal and commercial vehicles.

Benefits of Car Wraps in Phoenix


  • UV Protection: Vinyl wraps act as a protective shield against Phoenix’s intense sunlight, preserving your vehicle’s finish.

  • Cost-Effective Makeover: Wraps offer an aesthetic transformation at a lower cost compared to paint jobs.

  • Custom Designs: Wraps allow endless style options from matte finishes to bold business graphics.

  • Paint Protection: They guard against scratches, chips, and desert dust.

  • Easy Maintenance: A hand wash or light rinse is all it takes to keep them clean.

  • Resale Value: Original paint remains intact, helping preserve resale value.

Types of Car Wraps


  • Full Wrap: Covers the entire vehicle—perfect for major makeovers or full branding.

  • Partial Wrap: Covers selected areas like hoods or doors for subtle upgrades or budget-friendly branding.

  • Color Change Wraps: Easily change your car’s color without repainting.

  • Printed Wraps: Display business logos or unique graphic designs.

  • Textured Wraps: Get textures like carbon fiber, satin, or brushed metal.
